Validation of basic email elements when composing an email

When composing an email message some basic validation should occur. This warns the user if they are about to send an email with no subject, no email body, or email a badly formed recepient address

Address, Subject, Email body valdation

Recipient Subject Email body Outcome message
validAddress@mailinator.com Test subject Test email body Sent
invalidAddress Test subject Test email body Not Sendable
Test subject Test email body Not Sendable
validAddress@mailinator.com Test email body Send
validAddress@mailinator.com Test subject Sent
